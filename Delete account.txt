Steps to delete your user account or specific user-related data for FC Rider or FC Driver app:

1. Email your request to us on: support@aspiringtech.online
2. Clearly explain your request i.e. if you would like to have your account removed OR if you would like some specific data to be removed.
3. Include your phone number for account (user ID) identification, verification and processing.
4. Allow us some time to process your request.
5. Receive a feedback on your request via email.
